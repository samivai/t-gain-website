Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your Weightoo account and personal data (Apple / Google requirement)

You can permanently delete your Weightoo account and all associated personal data at any time. Deletion is irreversible — your weight logs, measurements, progress photos, habit history, AI coach conversations, and profile will be removed and cannot be restored.

Option 1 — Inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Weightoo and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion prompt.
  4. You will be signed out immediately. Your data is deleted within 30 days.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you can't access the app, email tgain.app@gmail.com from the email address linked to your Apple or Google sign-in account. Include the word "Delete account" in the subject line. We will verify your identity, delete your account within 30 days, and email you a confirmation.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (sex, age, height, weight goal, target date, preferences).
  • All weight logs, body measurements, and progress photos.
  • All habit completions, streaks, daily check-ins, and check-in notes.
  • All AI coach conversations.
  • Notification tokens and device identifiers tied to your account.

What may be retained

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics that can no longer identify you.
  • Subscription receipts and billing records required by tax and consumer-protection law, kept for the legally required period.
  • Records required to prevent fraud or comply with a legal request.

Important — cancel your subscription separately

Deleting your Weightoo account does not automatically cancel an active App Store or Google Play subscription. To stop future billing, cancel it from the store before or after deletion:

  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Weightoo → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Google Play → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Weightoo → Cancel.
